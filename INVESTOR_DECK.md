# campus2corporate (c2c)
**The Ultimate Ordeal for Future Legends**

---

## 1. The Problem: The "Raw Talent" Gap
The modern transition from campus to corporate is fundamentally broken.
*   **For Students:** High anxiety, "ghosting" culture, and a lack of understanding of what startups and enterprises actually need (resilience, social influence, not just code). They have technical skills but lack the "Professional Legend."
*   **For Universities (TPOs):** Training & Placement Offices (TPOs) lack granular, actionable data on cohort readiness. They cannot easily identify which students need resilience training versus leadership coaching.
*   **For Employers:** Resumes are dead. Employers need verified proof of *how* a candidate handles adversity, pivots, and group dynamics—not just what degree they hold.

## 2. The Solution: The "Scientific Makeover"
**c2c** is an AI-first recruitment and branding suite that transforms raw technical brilliance into enterprise-ready impact.
We don't just match resumes; we run candidates through an intelligent, multi-dimensional **"Ordeal."**

Our platform provides:
1.  **AI-Driven Psychometrics:** A proprietary 500-question bank measuring IQ, EQ, SQ, AQ, and SpQ.
2.  **The Ordeal Assessment:** Randomized, high-pressure situational judgment tests mapping students to "Founder Profiles" (Builder, Leader, Rainmaker, Anchor).
3.  **Automated Branding:** Generating a verified "Professional Legend" (interactive portfolio) upon completion.
4.  **Real-Time Market Intel:** Autonomous scraping of high-signal AI/Tech roles from niche boards (HN, RemoteOK) to instantly map certified students to open roles.

## 3. The Product Ecosystem (Live & Functional)
We have built a fully integrated, multi-tenant SaaS platform:
*   **Student Gateway:** Onboarding, Assessment Engine, and Personal Development Dashboard.
*   **Educator/TPO Portal:** Cohort Intelligence Heatmaps, Risk & Support Needs flagging, and Founder Profile distributions.
*   **Market Scout Agent:** A 24/7 autonomous agent enriching job leads via Gemini AI and storing them in Supabase.
*   **Central Orchestrator:** The API nervous system bridging student profiles with live market demands.

## 4. Why Now? (Timing & Market)
*   **The AI Shift:** Generative AI is displacing entry-level coding tasks. Employers now hire for adaptability (AQ) and emotional intelligence (EQ).
*   **The Gen Z Context:** Students are facing unprecedented anxiety regarding AI displacement and remote work dynamics. c2c speaks directly to these modern stressors.
*   **The Death of the Resume:** The market demands verifiable portfolios and behavioral metrics over static PDFs.

## 5. Business Model & Revenue Streams
B2B2C Multi-Tenant SaaS.
*   **Tier 1: University/TPO Licensing (B2B):** Annual subscriptions for colleges to access the TPO Dashboard, cohort analytics, and assessment seats for their students.
*   **Tier 2: Hiring Partner Access (B2B):** "Placement-as-a-Service." Employers pay to access the ranked, verified "Professional Legends" and the AI-curated pipeline.
*   **Tier 3: Premium Student Tools (B2C Upsell):** Advanced interview prep, 1-on-1 AI coaching (Campus-to-Corporate makeover), and premium portfolio hosting.

## 6. Traction & Moat
*   **Proprietary Data Asset:** 500+ custom-engineered psychometric items specifically targeting Gen Z contexts (ghosting, async work, hackathons).
*   **Technical Moat:** Fully functional Next.js + FastAPI Serverless architecture deployed on Vercel with Supabase backend.
*   **Agentic Framework:** We aren't just an app; we are an orchestration of specialized AI agents (Anthropologist, Psychologist) that process talent dynamically.

# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Phase 1: The "Lighthouse" Cohorts (Months 1-3)
**Objective:** Validate the platform, gather initial data, and build case studies.
*   **Target:** 3-5 forward-thinking Engineering and Business colleges (Tier 2/3 institutions looking to boost placement rates).
*   **Tactic:** Offer the platform for free to the current graduating batch.
*   **Key Result:** 1,000+ completed Ordeal assessments, generating the first Cohort Heatmaps for TPOs and refining the AI scoring logic.

## Phase 2: The "Talent Drop" & Employer Seeding (Months 3-6)
**Objective:** Prove the placement value and monetize the employer side.
*   **Target:** Early-stage startups, AI labs, and mid-market tech firms.
*   **Tactic:** Use our "Market Scout" agent to identify companies actively hiring. Present them with a curated, pre-assessed list of "Professional Legends" (Builders, Rainmakers) for free.
*   **Key Result:** Successful placements attributed to c2c, generating powerful testimonials from both students and employers.

## Phase 3: B2B Scaling & Land-and-Expand (Months 6-12)
**Objective:** Monetize TPOs and scale institution onboarding.
*   **Target:** Training & Placement Officers (TPOs) nationwide.
*   **Tactic:** Leverage the success stories from Phase 2. "Your students are getting hired because they passed the Ordeal." Sell the annual SaaS license for the TPO Dashboard so they can proactively intervene with students exhibiting high "Risk & Support Needs."
*   **Key Result:** 20+ paid university contracts.

## Phase 4: Product-Led Growth (PLG) & The Network Effect (Year 2+)
**Objective:** Viral student adoption.
*   **Tactic:** The "Professional Legend" portfolio generated by c2c becomes a status symbol. Students share their "Founder Fit" (e.g., "I'm a Rainmaker!") and radar charts on LinkedIn. 
*   **Key Result:** Organic inbound requests from students to take the assessment, forcing their universities to adopt the platform or allowing us to open a direct B2C funnel.
